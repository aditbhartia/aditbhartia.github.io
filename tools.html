Tools that provide step function improvements in my quality of life

What do I mean by quality of life?

Being able to spend more of your focus on the items that you want to and less on stuff that you don't really care about.

Without further ado...
  • EternalTerminal - I don't want to think about reconnecting to a remote session every time I open my laptop. I don't even want to be aware that the terminal is attached to a machine somewhere else. I want to open my laptop and work without having to spend my time focusing on the machine where I'm doing my work. Once I installed EternalTerminal, I had to think about one less thing regarding my workflow and could focus more on the problem I'm working on.
  • OSCYank - Similar theme to EternalTerminal, but with a focus on Vim. Yanking and pasting to/from a remote machine shouldn't feel any different than yanking/pasting the way I'm used to. Having to think about whether I'm yanking from a local or remote machine and change my behavior is something that isn't particularly interesting. A gamechanger for copying data back and forth from a remote machine.
Let me know if you've found anything that falls under this theme! Any tool that now seems unfathomable to go without on a daily basis fits.
